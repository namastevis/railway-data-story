8,477 was never really about stations.

It's a map of where the Indian state chose, or was able, to show up - dense where the Gangetic plain and colonial trunk lines already ran deep, thin across the mountainous frontier, and concentrated, almost absurdly, around a single financial capital. Every category code, every duplicate row, every two-letter name is a small fact. Put together, they're a portrait of a country still being written, one station at a time.
